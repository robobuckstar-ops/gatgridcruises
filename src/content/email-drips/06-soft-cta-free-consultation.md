### What a free consultation actually looks like

A 20–30 minute call (or email thread if you prefer — no pressure on phones).
We'll cover:

- **Your travel window** — flexible dates often unlock 20–40% savings
- **Your party size and ages** — drives ship choice more than people realize
- **Your priorities** — first-timer, returning cruiser, special occasion,
  multi-generational
- **Your budget shape** — what's realistic for verandah vs. oceanview vs.
  inside, plus the often-overlooked ancillary costs

At the end, we'll pull together **two or three concrete sailing options**
with current pricing and the onboard credit we can attach. You walk away
with a clear comparison sheet. You decide what to do next.

### What it costs

Nothing. Disney pays our commission, not you. The cruise fare is identical
whether you book through us or direct — we just layer onboard credit and
hands-on help on top.

### What it doesn't include

- High-pressure follow-up
- Pushy phone calls
- "This deal expires in 24 hours" tactics
- A pitch for cruise insurance you didn't ask about

If after the consultation it's not a fit, that's totally fine. Plenty of
folks read the guide and book direct. We'd rather help the right families
than every family.
